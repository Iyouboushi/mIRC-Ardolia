!look
Shows current room description and exits.  Does not count towards the dungeon action limit

!go [north, south, east, west, up, down]
Moves the party to the direction. Counts as a dungeon action.

!rest
The party recovers 50% of their HP and MP.  Has a chance of causing an event to occur afterwards.  Counts as a dungeon action

!warp
Ends the dungeon. Will happen automatically if all 30 dungeon actions have been used. 

!pull/!push/!open/!close/!read
All cost 1 adventure action and will interact with an object in the room (including chests)
